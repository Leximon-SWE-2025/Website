Leximon

Leximon is a top-down turn-based battle game where players use their knowledge of synonyms and antonyms to win against the computer-controlled enemies. Victory will allow the player to level up and gain access to new words. In order to keep the game exciting, the player will be presented with a random selection of words that they know. Due to the structure of the game, students can fight a few battles during their breaks at school or sit down for longer sessions of Leximon during their free time.

Project Background

Leximon was designed for students who are in the latter end of elementary school. We wanted to create a game that would reinforce their learning about synonyms and antonyms of different words.
